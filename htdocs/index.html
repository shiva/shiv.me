The longer version

I grew up in Pondicherry, India, studied at PEC, and eventually made my way to Vancouver, BC — where I've been for over a decade. I write code, take photographs, read a lot, and occasionally attempt to play the piano.

I'm a hacker in the original sense — not security, but the build-things-for-the-love-of-it kind. I've shipped operating systems, databases, DSLs, iOS apps, and web infrastructure. I'm most interested in systems programming, language design, and the craft of building software that lasts.

Outside of work: photography (film and digital), tennis, reading — especially history, science, and long-form nonfiction.
